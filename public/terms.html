5. Data Retention & Privacy

EchoValue is designed to be ephemeral:

  • All data is stored in memory only — no database, no disk writes
  • Sessions and all captured requests are automatically deleted after 24 hours
  • We do not collect, log, or share any personal information
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • IP addresses may be captured as part of request metadata and are visible only to the session owner. They are deleted with the session.

Do not send sensitive data (passwords, tokens, personal information) through EchoValue. Sessions are not encrypted at rest and URLs are not secret — anyone with the session URL can view captured requests.

6. Fair Usage

To ensure the service remains available for everyone, the following technical limits apply:

  • Maximum 100 requests stored per session (older requests are discarded)
  • Maximum 1 MB body size per request (larger bodies are truncated)
  • Sessions expire after 24 hours of inactivity
